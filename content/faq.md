## Is this site really free?

Yes. Every tutorial is free to read. We may show ads to cover hosting.

## Do I need an account?

No. Your progress is stored in your browser and never leaves your device.

## How do I sync progress across devices?

You can't — there's no backend. If you need cross-device sync, you'll have
to remember where you were.

## How are tutorials organized?

The hierarchy is **Track → Course → Chapter**. Tracks are big-picture
curricula; courses are focused topics; chapters are individual lessons.

## Can I contribute?

Send us an email — see the [contact page](/contact/).
