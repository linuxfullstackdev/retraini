## Summary

We don't run user accounts and we don't store personal data on a server.
Reading progress and font preferences are saved in your browser via
`localStorage` and never leave your device.

## Cookies

The site itself does not set tracking cookies. Third-party advertising
slots, when present, may set their own cookies — those are governed by the
ad provider's privacy policy.

## Analytics

If analytics is enabled, only anonymized, aggregate metrics are collected.
We do not attempt to identify individual readers.
